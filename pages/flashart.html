Flash Art Cinema: Dine-In Theatre Concept

Flash Art Cinema is a well known outdoor cinema cafe based in the heart of Orlando, Fl. They were seeking an updated website and branding system that connected better with their target audience that had been built over the last 7 years. Over much thought, going with a retro, bright cinema vibe was the best decision. This concept was brought on by a project from my CSS Design class. Classmates and I were intstructed to create a Dine-In Theatre concept that needed a brand identity and full workup on the website. We were instructed to create and design a home, now showing, movie, and menu page.
