Pemburtons Whisky Alliance - Brand Design Concept

Pemburtons Whisky Alliance is a resturant concept that specializes in serving the finest whiskey along-side gourmet burgers. They are a speakeasy styled gastropub with intent on marketing towards higher-end clients who are willing to emerse themselves in a good evening. This project was for my Vector Illustration course and the goal was to create a complete, single logo and apply the logo to branding mockups. For this assignment we were randomly assigned the color pallette and had to make it work on our design. Since Pemburtons is a speakeasy as well as an alliance, I decided to use a handshake to represent the company.
