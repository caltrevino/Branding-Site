Son of a Bun - Brand Design Concept

Son of a Bun is a bakery concept located in Orlando Fl. They were seeking an updated logo that felt modern and hip. The client wasn't looking for anything cheesy and described their business as "Orlando's Hometown Bakery". Taking this into consideration I decided to use a warmer color palette and used a modern font that also didn't feel too harsh. I was to create a full logo and branding system for Son of a Bun. This entailed a new logo, business card design, newsletter, and coporate letter. This brand design was a project for the Graphic Design Essentials course in my degree.
